RESEARCH INTERESTS

I am a data analysis and machine learning specialist with over a decade of experience in applying novel and cutting edge methods to interesting scientific questions.
My research is currently applied to tumour evolution, with a specific focus on lung cancer in never-smokers.

I have previously worked in Brain-Computer Interfaces (BCI), analysing electroencephalogram (EEG) data with the aim of improving quality of life for people with severe motor disabilities.

If the question is interesting, and it could help improve lives, you can bet I'll be interested.
